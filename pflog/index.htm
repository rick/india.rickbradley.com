pflog

A Flash travel map utility by Erik Frey.

The web site for pflog is http://fawx.com/software/pflog/.

pflog is released under the terms of the zlib/libpng License.

Revision History

0.8.2
Two new features, one bug fix:
  • Moved scrollbar up onto map.
  • pflog now scales nicely to any size.
  • Embedded fonts to address Flash for Linux not rendering _sans.
0.8.1
Two small fixes:
  • Small recalibration to make coordinates more accurate on high zoom.
  • Fixed gotodate from occasionally not zooming as expected.
  • Fixed Flash's excessive caching tendencies.
0.8
Initial Release.
